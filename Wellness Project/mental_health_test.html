Depression

Symptoms: Lack of interest, fatigue, sadness.
Feelings: Hopelessness, isolation.
Care: Therapy, support groups, medication.

Anxiety

Symptoms: Restlessness, overthinking.
Feelings: Nervousness, fear.
Care: Relaxation techniques, CBT, healthy routine.

PTSD

Symptoms: Flashbacks, avoidance.
Feelings: Distress after trauma.
Care: Trauma therapy, support systems.

ADHD

Symptoms: Impulsivity, inattention.
Feelings: Frustration, scattered thoughts.
Care: Routine, focus tools, expert help.

Youth Mental Health

Issues: Peer pressure, identity crisis.
Feelings: Confusion, loneliness.
Care: Family talks, mentorship, healthy expression.
